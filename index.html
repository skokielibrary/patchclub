Patch Club Skokie Public Library

The Reading Patch Club is an exciting year-round reading program especially for kids in preschool through 8th grade.

Download your log

Three to five year olds

Three to five year olds can earn four colorful patches by reading 50 books. Select titles from our Good Books for 4-5 year-olds, or ask a librarian for recommendations. Return to the library after reading 5, then 10, then 15, and then 20 books to receive your patches.

Kindergarten through 8th grade

Students in kindergarten through 8th grade can earn patches for reading ten books in a genre. Check out the current Reading Patch Club genres (and the patches, too!)…
